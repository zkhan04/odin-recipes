PB&J sandwich

Description

The PBJ is a staple recipe for anyone who's bulking. Its simplicity - just three ingredients and two minutes of prep time - is what makes it such a widely-loved classic.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • 2 slices of bread

Steps

  1. spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  3. put them together!
  4. Optional: toast the sandwich in a toaster oven
