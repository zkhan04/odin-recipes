Chocolate Peanut Butter Protein Shake

Description

Protein shakes are great because of how easy they are to prepare, and the nutritional value you get from them. This is the recipe I personally use, which has roughly 35-37g protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 scoops protein powder
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter

Steps

  1. Pour the milk into a blender
  2. Drop the peanut butter into the blender
  3. Drop 2 scoops of protein powder into the blender
  4. Blend and enjoy!
